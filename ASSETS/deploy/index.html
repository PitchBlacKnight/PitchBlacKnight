About

Mikel Rosenthal

Mikel Rosenthal is a polymath and Fluxus artist working across music, performance, visual art, design, and voice. He makes music while he works, records it by accident, and calls that a method. He is ambidextrous, hyperfantasiac, and can replicate any sound he hears — human, animal, mechanical — usually on the first try.

Daily Wisdom

Today's Transmission

"The most radical act is to be entirely, unapologetically yourself in a world that profits from your self-doubt."
I learned to edit before I spoke
to think twice before I felt
to fold myself into rooms
that were not built for my wingspan.
But compression is not peace.
And now the thaw —
not explosive, not dramatic,
just: a door left open
that I forgot I had the key to.
