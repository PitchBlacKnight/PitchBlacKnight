04 — Daily Wisdom

"The most radical act is to be entirely, unapologetically yourself in a world that profits from your self-doubt."

— PitchBlackNight

I have been every version of myself

that the moment demanded.

Not because I lacked a center,

but because my center is motion.

The canvas does not ask what brush you used.

The song does not remember the key you started in.

Only the result speaks —

and it speaks in frequencies

the mind forgot it could hear.

Refuse the lane.

Refuse the label.

Refuse the version of you

that fits neatly in the box

they built before you arrived.
